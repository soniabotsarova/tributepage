Xavier Dolan

"A movie that is unable to elicit emotion isn't a movie."

Things you need to know about Xavier Dolan:
  • He has been acting since the age of four.
  • At the age of 20, he wrote and directed his first feature film, I Killed My Mother.
  • I Killed My Mother was followed by Heartbeats and Laurence Anyways.
  • Both films were selected for Un Certain Regard at the Festival de Cannes.
  • In 2013, his fourth film Tom at the Farm was awarded the FIPRESCI Prize.
  • His film Mommy has also garnered numerous awards, including the Jury Prize at last year’s Festival de Cannes and the César for Best Foreign Feature.

Filmography

I kiled my mother (2009)

A semi-autobiographical story about Hubert as a young homosexual at odds with his mother.
Heartbeats (2010)

The story of three close friends who are involved in a love-triangle.
Lawrence Anyways (2012)

A drama that charts ten years in a transgender woman's relationship with her lover.

Mommy (2014)

A widowed single mother, raising her violent son alone, finds new hope when a mysterious neighbor inserts herself into their household.
It's Only the End of the World (2016)

Louis (Gaspard Ulliel), a terminally ill writer, returns home after a long absence to tell his family that he is dying.
Matthias & Maxime (2019)

A drama focusing on a group of friends in their late 20s.
